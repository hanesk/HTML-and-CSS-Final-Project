Prep Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

Lembas Bread
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • 2/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

    • Instructions

    1. To prepare the dough:
      1. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt into a large mixing bowl.
      2. Add the butter and mix well until the it becomes fine. Then mix in the brown sugar and cinnamon.
      3. Add the whipping cream, honey, and vanilla. Stir well with a fork until the dough forms.
    2. Forming the Lembas Bread
      1. Roll the dough out onto a baking mat.
      2. Use a square cookie cutter or a knife to seperate the dough into squares and place each piece onto a prepared baking sheet.
      3. Cut each piece diagonally from corner to corner with a knife carefully, but do not cut all the way through.
    3. Bake in an oven preheated to 425 degrees F for 10-12 minutes. More or less time may be needed depending on the thickness of the bread. Best served with honey.
