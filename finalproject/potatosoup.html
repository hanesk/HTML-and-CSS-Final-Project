The Prancing Pony's Potato Soup

Prep time: 45-60 minutes

Ingredients

Potato Soup
  • 4 tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 2 large, fresh garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 lbs or cubed potatoes or cubed hashbrowns
  • about 1 lb of broccoli
  • Kosher salt
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • Dried parsley seasoning

    • Instructions

    1. In a large pot, melt the butter on medium low heat and then add the crushed garlic and cook for 3-4 minutes.
    2. Then stir in the potatoes and chopped broccoli. Cover with a lid and cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
    3. Stir again. Then add the vegetable broth and a pinch of kosher salt. Bring to a boil.
    4. Once it's boiling, stir occasionally and simmer gently with the lid on for about 25 minutes.
    5. Stir in the milk and add a light dusting of parsley on top, stir again. Cook for about 5 more minutes.
    6. Let cool for a few minutes, then serve and enjoy!
