Sunflower and Pumpkin Seed Bread Rolls

Prep Time: 2 hrs

Ingredients

Bread Rolls
  • 1/4 oz active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water - not hot
  • 3 cups white bread flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 3 tbsp sunflower kernals
  • 3 tbsp pumpkin seeds
    • Egg Wash
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp water
  • a pinch of salt

    • Instructions

    1. To prepare the dough:
      1. Sprinkle the yeast over the measured warm water in a bowl. Stir well and set aside for about 10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
      2. In a seperate mixing bowl, sift the bread flour and salt. Add the cubes of butter and rub them in until the texure of the mixure resembles fine breadcrumbs.
      3. Add the pumpkin and sunflower seeds and stir.
      4. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon, then take the dough out of the bowl and knead with your hands for 5 minutes.
      5. When the dough feels firm and is no longer sticky, set aside back in the bowl and cover with a towel for 30 minutes to let it double in size.
    2. Shaping the dough:
      1. Turn the dough out of the bowl, punch it down, and knead again.
      2. Divide the dough into 12 pieces, kneading each piece briefly into a ball before setting aside.
      3. Set the rolls onto a lightly greased baking sheet, cover them with a towel, and let rise for another 30 minutes.
    3. Adding the egg wash
      1. In a small bowl, add the egg yolk, water, and a pinch of salt. Stir.
      2. Remove the towel from the bread rolls and use a brush to coat the tops of each roll with the egg mixture.
    4. Set the baking sheet into an oven preheated to 400 degrees F. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the tops are golden. The rolls should be hard on top when you tap them, but sound hollow. Let cool before serving. Best served with butter or as a side to soup.
